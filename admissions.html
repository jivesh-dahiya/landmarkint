Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and transparent. We encourage prospective students and their families to review the following steps:

  • Complete the online application form available on our website.
  • Submit the required documents, including previous academic records, recommendation letters, and identification.
  • Attend an interview with our admissions committee.
  • Receive notification of admission status via email.
  • Complete the enrollment process, including payment of fees and submission of additional documents.

Fee Structure

Our fee structure is designed to be competitive and transparent. We offer various payment plans to accommodate different financial situations. For detailed information on tuition fees, please refer to the fee structure document available on our website below.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for admission, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Age requirements as per the grade level.
  • Minimum academic performance standards.
  • Submission of all required documents by the specified deadlines.
  • Participation in the admission interview process.
